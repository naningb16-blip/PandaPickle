FRIEND GROUPS SCHEDULE - HOW IT WORKS
======================================

## OVERVIEW
Friend groups ARE included in match generation and GET game numbers.
The "Friend Groups Schedule" section shows when each group will play.

## EXAMPLE SCENARIO

### Registration:
- 4 teams register: "Birthday Party" group (select "Play with Friends")
- 4 teams register: "Company Outing" group (select "Play with Friends")
- 6 teams register: "Random Match" (select "Random Match")
- Total: 14 teams

### Admin Approves & Generates Matches:

When admin clicks "Generate Matches", the system:

**Step 1: Match Birthday Party teams**
- Team 1 vs Team 2 → Creates match with game_number = 1
- Team 3 vs Team 4 → Creates match with game_number = 2

**Step 2: Match Company Outing teams**
- Team 5 vs Team 6 → Creates match with game_number = 3
- Team 7 vs Team 8 → Creates match with game_number = 4

**Step 3: Match Random teams**
- Team 9 vs Team 10 → Creates match with game_number = 5
- Team 11 vs Team 12 → Creates match with game_number = 6
- Team 13 vs Team 14 → Creates match with game_number = 7

**Total: 7 matches created with game numbers 1-7**

## KEY POINTS

✅ Friend groups ARE generated into matches
✅ Friend groups GET game numbers (sequential: 1, 2, 3, 4...)
✅ Friend Groups Schedule shows WHICH games they'll play
✅ Generated Matches shows ALL matches with game numbers
✅ Admin can use game numbers to schedule courts

## BENEFITS

1. **Friend Groups Schedule** = Quick overview of which friend groups play when
   - See: "Birthday Party plays Game 1-2"
   - See: "Company Outing plays Game 3-4"

2. **Generated Matches** = Detailed match pairings with game numbers
   - See: "Game 1: John & Jane vs Mike & Sarah"
   - See: "Game 2: Tom & Lisa vs Bob & Alice"

3. **Court Scheduling** = Admin uses game numbers to organize
   - "Court 1: Game 1, then Game 3, then Game 5"
   - "Court 2: Game 2, then Game 4, then Game 6"

## BEFORE MATCH GENERATION

Friend Groups Schedule shows:
- Group name
- Teams count
- Team names
- "Not matched yet" (no game numbers)

## AFTER MATCH GENERATION

Friend Groups Schedule shows:
- Group name
- Teams count
- Team names
- **Game numbers** (e.g., "Game 1-2")
- Number of matches
