PLAY WITH FRIENDS - UPDATED FEATURE
====================================

## WHAT CHANGED

The "Play with Friends" feature has been completely redesigned based on your needs!

## OLD WAY (Wrong):
- Register only 2 players (you + partner)
- Enter a "friend group name"
- Hope your friends register separately with same name
- System matches you later

## NEW WAY (Correct!):
- Register ALL 4 PLAYERS at once!
- Team 1: Your name + Partner name
- Team 2: Friend 1 + Friend 2
- Pay PHP 200 total (both teams together)
- Creates complete match immediately!

## HOW IT WORKS NOW

### For Users (open-play.php):

**Option 1: Random Match (PHP 100)**
- Enter your name + partner name
- System will randomly match you with another team
- Pay PHP 100 for your team (2 players)

**Option 2: Play with Friends (PHP 200)**
- Enter Team 1: Your name + Partner name
- Enter Team 2: Friend 1 name + Friend 2 name
- All 4 players registered together!
- Pay PHP 200 total (all 4 players)
- You play together - no random matching needed!

## EXAMPLE SCENARIO

**You want to play with your friends:**

1. You open registration form
2. Select "Play with Friends"
3. Fill in:
   - Team 1: Derodd & Debie (you and your partner)
   - Team 2: Noel & Niel (your friends)
4. Select payment method
5. Click Register
6. System creates:
   - Registration 1: Derodd & Debie (PHP 100)
   - Registration 2: Noel & Niel (PHP 100)
   - Total: PHP 200
   - Both teams linked together automatically!

7. After admin approves:
   - System creates match: Derodd & Debie vs Noel & Niel
   - Gets assigned game number (e.g., Game 1)
   - You play together!

## BENEFITS

✅ Register all 4 players at once
✅ Pay PHP 200 total (correct amount)
✅ Don't need to coordinate with friends to register separately

✅ Creates complete match immediately

## ADMIN SIDE

When admin views registrations:
- Sees 2 team registrations with same friend_group ID
- Both teams show "Play with Friends" preference
- Both teams linked together automatically
- When generating matches, they're matched together
- Gets game number assigned

## PAYMENT

**Random Match:**
- PHP 100 per team (2 players)
- You pay for your team only

**Play with Friends:**
- PHP 200 total (4 players / 2 teams)
- Creates 2 separate registrations:
  * Team 1: PHP 100
  * Team 2: PHP 100
- Both need admin approval
- Both marked as paid when you pay PHP 200 total

## TECHNICAL DETAILS

- Both teams get same unique friend_group ID (e.g., "friends_abc123")
- Both teams linked together in database
- Match generation pairs them together
- They get sequential game number
- Can be tracked in Friend Groups Schedule
